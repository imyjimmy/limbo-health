Request an EHI Export

UW Medicine uses an electronic health record called Epic that is certified by the Office of the National Coordinator (ONC) Health IT Certification Program. The ONC Certification Program requires that electronic health information (EHI) stored in Epic to be made available to patients in a machine-readable format commonly referred to as “EHI Export.” To learn more about what an EHI Export would contain, or to request this, please use this form: Patient Authorization to Release a Machine-Readable Export of Protected Health Information.
